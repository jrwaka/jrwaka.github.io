Not sure what to play next? I've got you covered. Here are five games that have been 
living rent-free in my head lately — and honestly, they deserve a spot on your playlist too.

## 1. FC 26
The beautiful game just got more beautiful. FC 26 brings sharper gameplay, improved 
player movement, and Ultimate Team updates that'll have you grinding matches all night. 
Whether you're a career mode fan or a competitive UT player, there's something for everyone.

## 2. Call of Duty
Fast, chaotic, and stupidly fun. CoD never gets old — drop in, cause some mayhem, and 
somehow always come back for one more match. The new maps and loadout options keep 
things fresh no matter how long you've been playing.

## 3. Spider-Man 2
Swinging through New York never gets old, and Spider-Man 2 takes everything from the 
first game and cranks it up. The story hits hard, the combat is slick, and the open 
world is absolutely gorgeous. This one is a must-play, no question.

## 4. NBA 2K26
If you're a basketball fan, NBA 2K26 is basically a lifestyle at this point. MyCareer 
mode is as addictive as ever, and the on-court gameplay feels smoother than previous 
editions. Warning: you will lose track of time.

## 5. Madden NFL 26
Football season never ends when you've got Madden. Build your franchise, hit the field, 
and take your team all the way to the championship. A solid pick whether you're a 
casual fan or a die-hard football head.
