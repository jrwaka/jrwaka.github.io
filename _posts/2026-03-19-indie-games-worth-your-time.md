Sports games get a bad reputation in some gaming circles — "they're just roster updates," 
people say. But as someone who genuinely loves FC 26, NBA 2K26, and Madden NFL 26, I'm 
here to make the case for why sports titles deserve more respect.

## The Depth Is Real
People underestimate how deep sports games actually are. NBA 2K26's MyCareer mode alone 
has more RPG elements than some dedicated RPGs. FC 26's Ultimate Team is practically 
its own economy. There's layers to these games.

## The Competitive Scene Is Intense
Call of Duty and FC 26 both have massive competitive communities. Getting good at these 
games takes real skill, game sense, and practice. Anyone who says sports games are easy 
hasn't played online at a high level.

## They're Great for Playing with Friends
Nothing beats loading up NBA 2K26 or Madden with friends and running a franchise 
together. Spider-Man 2 is a great solo experience, but sports games shine brightest 
in a social setting.

## The Storytelling Has Improved
Spider-Man 2 proved that action games can tell incredible stories, and sports games 
are catching up. NBA 2K's story modes have gotten genuinely cinematic over the years, 
with real voice acting and cutscenes that pull you in.

## Every Year Brings Something New
Yes, annual releases get criticized — but every edition does bring meaningful updates. 
Better gameplay mechanics, new modes, improved graphics. If you haven't played in a 
couple of years, the difference is noticeable.

Sports games are for real gamers too. Don't let anyone tell you otherwise. 🎮
